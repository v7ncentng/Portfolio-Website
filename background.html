A bit about me:

Hello fellow internet user! My name is Vincent Ng and I was born in Hong Kong, and spent a majority of my life in the SF Bay area.

My journey into coding began at age 10 on platforms like Scratch and Code.org, where I built simple games like hangman, tag, and tic-tac-toe. The early curiosity stemmed from connecting my childhood to software and evolved into a passion for programming that still burns as I reach my third year in UC Davis.

I have been especially hooked on building my programming skillset through fullstack projects, with an emphasis on AI/machine learning and deep learning applications.

My passion continuously grows at UC Davis, where I'm studying Computer Science, Statistics, and Technology Management, creating projects intertwining my personal interests and user experience.

When I'm not behind a computer, I love to practice boxing, hike, lift weights, and explore new food cuisines!

I'm looking forward to connecting!
