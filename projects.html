Connect-4 Bot

AI-Powered Game Bot

×

I built FourSight, an intelligent Connect-4 bot powered by the minimax algorithm through alpha-beta pruning. Foursight analyzes game states up to 7 moves ahead and implements strategic heuristics to provide a challenging gaming experience for players of all skill levels. Users also have the option to play against random simulations, like MonteCarlo AI and Random AI, or play against each other!

Technologies Used:

Python Pygame NumPy React.js Alpha-Beta Pruning
